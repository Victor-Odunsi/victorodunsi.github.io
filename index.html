About Me

I am a data scientist and machine learning engineer with a strong foundation in analytical problem-solving and end-to-end model development. My background as a medical laboratory scientist has given me a deep appreciation for precision, rigor, and the real-world impact of data-driven decisions. Today, I specialize in Python, scikit-learn, PyTorch, and LangChain, leveraging these tools to build and deploy machine learning and AI solutions. My work spans classical machine learning, deep learning, and LLM-powered applications, with a strong focus on deploying models into production using modern frameworks. A core area of my passion is the application of ML and AI in healthcare. Many of my projects explore how data-driven insights and intelligent systems can improve diagnostics, enhance decision-making, and ultimately contribute to better patient outcomes. Beyond technical expertise, I prioritize clarity in communication—translating complex results into actionable insights that drive meaningful impact. I am committed to using AI not just as a technological tool, but as a force for solving real humanitarian and healthcare challenges.
